Project 02
Prediction Engine — Any Time Series
Cycle X IN DEV
Imagine knowing precisely where the 2020 COVID crash and its bottom would land. Or the 2024 correction. Or the 2025 drop. Cycle X does that. It applies a proprietary geometric transformation to price data, maps it into a coordinate space where market dynamics become linear and measurable, then uses feature engineering on historical swing structures to predict where the next one completes. Validated out of sample. Works on any time series, mainly equities.
